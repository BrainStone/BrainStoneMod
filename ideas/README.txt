                                   What is this folder all about?
====================================================================================================

    This folder contains all ideas that are good enough to possibly add them to the mod. The ideas
    will not have a specific format. They might be made out of images (word) documents, tables, ...
    Therefore every major (and maybe minor too) idea will have its own folder. Every very small idea
    will find its place in the "misc" folder.
    
    The files in the idea folder will be displayed in gource unless they're taking up too much
    space!
